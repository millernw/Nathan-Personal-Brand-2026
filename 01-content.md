- **Subheadline:** Nathan Miller helps founders and executives get clarity on what they're saying, build systems that say it consistently, and create marketing that compounds over time.
- **Primary CTA button:** Work with me
- **Secondary CTA:** Read my writing
- **Hero visual description:** Large, editorial portrait of Nathan — cropped dramatically, high contrast, slightly overlapping with headline text for a layered effect. The image should feel like a magazine cover, not a headshot. Use a parallax scroll effect so the image moves at a different rate than the text as the user scrolls down.

## Value Propositions

1. **Your message is your leverage** — Most businesses don't have a marketing problem — they have a clarity problem. When your message is sharp, everything else gets easier: ads convert, referrals stick, and sales shorten.
2. **Systems replace hustle** — Posting more isn't a strategy. Nathan builds automated marketing systems that nurture leads, follow up consistently, and generate pipeline without adding to your workload.
3. **Strategy before tactics** — No more guessing which platform to be on or what content to create. Every move is grounded in a clear strategic foundation with measurable outcomes.
4. **Built for small business** — Nathan works exclusively with founders and small business operators who are doing real revenue and are ready to scale smart — not with startups chasing vanity metrics.
